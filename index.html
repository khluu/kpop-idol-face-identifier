How to use

Pick an image of the KPop idol that you want to identify and paste the image address into the box above and hit the button.
You also have the option to upload the picture from your device.

Note: It only works for the idols listed below. Since it takes a while to train the system to identify faces and I can't afford the cost to run the API, I only included the most famous groups. The accuracy of this identifier can be guaranteed at 85-90%. If you put in someone not on the list, the program may try to identify he/she as someone else similar.

Why I made this website

I believe it's really a thing among me and my friends: We usually can't identify KPop idols ourselves since they look a lot similar (except for the most popular ones)
Therefore, I came up with an idea of making a face identifier to help myself and the homies.
